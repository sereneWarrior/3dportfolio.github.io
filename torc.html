FINISHED

Waldalgesheim torc

The reference

A torc is a piece of jewelery typical for european iron age. The piece I used as reference is from a 4th-century BC dated burial in Waldalgesheim, Germany. Since I really love the art style that established in middle europe during iron age I decided to create a digital replica.

The process

My first idea was to sclupt the onamants but then I came across the mask extraction tool in Blender. So, I masked the ornament structure on the ring, extracted it an used the solidify modifier.

The result

I tried to give the resulting ornaments a more organic form using sculpting tools. But the results turned out too bumpy. So, I decided to start over again this time to model them. The output is not only less heavy in geometry (Ihad to subdevide the base ring a few times to be able to get smooth result in the mask painting) it looks cleaner as well.
